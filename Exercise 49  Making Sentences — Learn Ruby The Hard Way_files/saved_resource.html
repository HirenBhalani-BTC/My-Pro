            When I wanted to test the parse_sentence method in this exercise, assert_equal kept showing a failure even though the two sentences I was comparing looked the same other than that they were different objects.

            So to make the assert_equal work, I went in and customized the "==" method for the Sentence class. I put this at the beginning of the class:

            attr_reader :subject
            attr_reader :verb
            attr_reader :object

            def ==(other)
            self.subject == other.subject && self.verb == other.verb && self.object == other.object
            end

            Am I missing something here, or is that okay to do? Is there a different assert method that would have been better to use without having to modify the original code?
